A professional and versatile sound engineer and composer specialising in compositional sound design and production sound mixing. Based in the UK. 4 years of experience with pre/post-production of sound. Experienced in all aspects of production, recording, composing, mixing and mastering in a range of projects, including films, documentaries, adverts, and musical theatre shows.

Their work has spread internationally with some of their compositions being performed by local orchestras, as well as their sound design being showcased in theatre shows around the UK. On top of this, they have worked as a session musician performing on a wide range of instruments in a variety of genres for a wider variety of clients worldwide.

Their work also spreads to spatial audio, specifically based on location recording, with extensive experience with ambisonic and binaural recording, as well as experience mixing and mastering with Dolby Atmos and other surround sound techniques.
